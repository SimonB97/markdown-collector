Privacy Policy

Data Collection and Storage

This extension is designed with privacy in mind:

  • All data is stored locally in your browser
  • I do not collect or transmit any personal data
  • No analytics or tracking is implemented
  • The extension only requests necessary permissions to function
  • Data is never transmitted outside your browser unless you explicitly enable and use the LLM feature
  • You can export or delete your data at any time

OpenAI Integration (Optional Feature)

When you enable the LLM (Language Model) feature and don't customize the endpoint:

  • Content is sent to OpenAI's servers for processing only when you explicitly choose to refine content
  • Your OpenAI API key is stored locally and is never transmitted to my servers
  • This processing is subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy
  • You can disable this feature at any time
  • You are responsible for complying with OpenAI's terms of service when using your API key
  • The extension does not validate or process the content returned from OpenAI
  • Custom endpoints: If you use a custom API endpoint, you are responsible for the security and privacy of that connection

Browser Permissions

The extension requires certain permissions to function:

  • Access to active tabs - to convert webpage content
  • Storage permission - to save markdown locally
  • Clipboard access - for copy functionality

Your Rights

As all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your data:

  • You can view all stored data in your browser's storage
  • You can delete all data at any time through your browser settings
  • You can export your data through the extension's interface
  • No data recovery is possible after deletion as I don't keep any backups

Updates and Changes

I reserve the right to update this privacy policy. Any changes will be reflected in this page. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
